# RentCan Premium Swiss Design System

A fusion of International Typographic Style (Swiss) and Scandinavian minimalism.

## Principles
1. **Grid Authority**: Rigid 12-column grids for all layouts.
2. **High-Contrast Type**: Bold, flush-left headings with tight tracking.
3. **Purposeful Accents**: Use 'Sage Green' for trust and 'Dusty Rose' for conversion.
4. **Immersive Media**: Videos and photos framed with precise 1px borders and generous padding.
