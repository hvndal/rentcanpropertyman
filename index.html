Good to know

Questions, answered.

Everything you might wonder before you download.

Do I have to be in India to use RentCan?
Not at all. Manage your property from anywhere in the world — your tenants use the app locally in the Tricity, and you stay in control from your phone.
How do tenants pay rent?
Through the app via Razorpay — UPI, cards, or netbanking. You're notified the moment rent is paid, and reminders go out automatically before each due date.
What if a tenant pays late?
RentCan sends automatic reminders ahead of the due date, and your dashboard shows payment status at a glance so nothing slips through the cracks.
Is there a setup fee or lock-in?
No setup fee and no lock-in — cancel anytime. Your first month is free, and you don't need a card to start.
Can I manage more than one property?
Yes — one dashboard for all of them. Your plan covers 1-2 properties; each extra one is ₹1,000/month, and any vacant unit drops to ₹499/month.
Is RentCan a broker?
No. RentCan helps with listing, coordination and paperwork, but we're not a real estate broker, agent or property manager unless agreed in writing Ã¢â‚¬â€ and you always make the final tenant decision. Full terms live in our Service Agreement.
