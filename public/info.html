The Process

From sign-up to hands-off.

Getting started takes less than 10 minutes. Here's exactly what happens after you create your account.

1

Create your account

Sign in with Google or your phone number — no password needed. This takes under 30 seconds.

2

Add your property

Enter your property address, type, and expected rent. Upload your lease and tenant documents into the secure document vault. We'll review everything.

3

One of our team members meets you (or your tenant) in person to collect a spare key set. This is essential for inspection access. Our team is based in Phase 7, Mohali.
