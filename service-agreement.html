01What RentCan does

RentCan is a property-management software and support service. We give owners a dashboard to track rent, requests and inspections, give tenants an app to pay and raise requests, and — on the Premium plan — coordinate maintenance through verified, outsourced technicians.

RentCan is a service provider to the property owner. We act on your instructions and within the limits you set. We are not the owner, landlord or lessor of any property, and we do not take possession or control of your premises.

03Payments & rent

Rent and fees are collected in-app through Razorpay. Available methods and how charges work:

  • UPI — the primary method, with no added processing charge to the tenant.
  • Netbanking — available as a secondary method.
  • Credit cards — may be disabled by default. Where enabled, a convenience fee of roughly 1.5%–2% may be added to the tenant’s invoice to cover card costs.
  • International cards — require manual activation and settle on a longer timeline (about T+7 business days, versus T+3 locally).

RentCan collects rent on the owner’s behalf and remits it according to your settlement settings. We are not a bank, lender or escrow agent, and we do not guarantee that any tenant will pay. Owners remain responsible for setting rent, due dates and any late-payment terms in the rental agreement.

04Maintenance & SOS

Maintenance coordination is part of the Premium plan. When a tenant or owner raises an on-request maintenance job:

  • RentCan dispatches a verified, outsourced technician.
  • Your monthly Premium fee covers dispatch and basic visit/diagnostic labour only (typically capped around ₹300–₹500 per visit).
  • If a part needs replacing, the technician uploads a photo and the app sends you an approval request — for example, “Approve ₹1,200 repair? Yes / No.” Nothing is charged for parts or major work without your approval.
  • You can switch on an auto-approve setting for urgent repairs under a limit you choose (for example, under ₹1,500), so small fixes don’t wait on a call.

Spare parts, major repairs and any work beyond basic diagnostic labour are billed separately and are the owner’s responsibility. RentCan coordinates technicians but does not itself perform construction, electrical, plumbing or other licensed trade work, and is not liable for the workmanship of third-party technicians beyond reasonable vendor selection.

05Monthly inspection

Active plans include one physical inspection per month against a 20-point checklist covering common areas, kitchen, bedrooms and bathrooms — with photos required on key items (locks, taps, AC, geyser and similar). Inspections are a visual condition check to flag issues early; they are not a structural, legal or safety certification, and they do not replace professional surveys where those are required.

06Vacancy Resolution Service

When a property becomes vacant, RentCan can assist the owner through the transition from vacancy to occupancy. This is a one-time service starting at ₹1,999. Services may include:

  • Vacancy assessment
  • Property listing assistance
  • Lead coordination and applicant collection
  • Tenant document collection and identity verification assistance
  • Employment, income and reference verification assistance
  • Viewing coordination
  • Move-in preparation, tenant onboarding support and move-in documentation

The goal is to reduce vacancy periods and simplify onboarding.

Important. RentCan does not act as a real estate broker, property manager or leasing agent unless expressly agreed in writing. RentCan does not guarantee tenant placement, occupancy timelines, applicant quality, rent collection, future tenant conduct, or lease performance.

Final tenant selection remains solely the responsibility of the property owner.

07Cancellation & refunds

There is no setup fee and no lock-in — you can cancel any time from your account, and your plan stops renewing at the end of the current billing month.

  • Your first month of Premium is free; if you cancel during the free month, you are not charged.
  • Monthly fees are billed in advance and are generally non-refundable for a month already started, since the service (rent handling, inspection, support) runs across that month.
  • One-time fees (tenant onboarding, Vacancy Resolution) are non-refundable once the work has begun.
  • Where a payment fails or is duplicated, contact us and we’ll sort it out.
